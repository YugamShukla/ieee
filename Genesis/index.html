Genesis

Genesis is the annual technical fest of IEEE

Inaugral Day

The first event was Elixir which was the unveiling of Genesis. The event was lots of dance and singing performances ,the vibe was great and people enjoyed themselves and the Genesis was announced open. The Genesis kicked off after the inaugural by a talk by none other than Ankush Singhla , the founder of Coding Ninjas. He took an interactive session

The first event was the Codeathon. Enthusiastic and brilliant coders took part in the event to make it a huge success. The event concluded with the winning teams but it was a very close competition among teams.

Day 2

The next day started with the Robonomics workshop. Students made a object avoiding bot all on their own and all the participants were utmost satisfied with the workshop and got to learn alot.

The later part of the day saw the Ethical hacking workshop cause hacking sounds so cool. The speaker was a very knowledgeable person and tried to deliver as much as possible of his knowledge to the students.

The Final Day!

Next day started with the Blockchain workshop which also saw a housefull participation. The Blockchain technology was explained in a very simple manner by the speaker and students could understand it with ease.After so many technical events , came Alterworld the gaming event with Fifa and Pubg tournament. All the gamers of the college could be seen at one place competing with each other for the prize money and enjoying themselves out.

The Genesis concluded with Symphony the musical concert by none other than the band Nishaad. Students enjoyed singing and dancing on the beats of the band. The Genesis was a three day fest which was very successful and gave platform to enjoy and learn to the students.
