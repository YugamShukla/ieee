About IEEE SB MUJ

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for humanity

IEEE Student Branch, Manipal University Jaipur, strives to create and spread awareness of the various technologies that surround us, promote research among students, and recognize technological excellence. Our vision to create a community of competent individuals that are passionate about technology has manifested itself in the form of endeavours such as monthly coding competitions, workshops, conferences, and seminars on the most pressing and relevant topics of our times. We have grown into a multi-faceted chapter, empowering young engineers to enhance their skills and set up milestones. Because of our dedication and ethos, IEEE SB MUJ has become one of the most active and influential student branches in the Rajasthan Circuit.

Moreover, the success of IEEE SB MUJ isn’t only limited to collective achievements; our members have won multiple awards of excellence across various IEEE International Events. We offer numerous possibilities to grow, explore, and learn.

IEEE is the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.

Other Societies

  • IEEE Computer Society MUJ

    IEEE Computer Society, Manipal University Jaipur, endeavours to advance the theory, practice, and application of computer and information processing science and technology. IEEE CS MUJ organizes conferences, workshops, seminars, and technical competitions to encourage professional interaction and lifelong learning; recognize outstanding contributions of individuals; support research in Computer Science and Information Technology.

  • IEEE WIE MUJ

    IEEE Women in Engineering, Affinity Group, Manipal University Jaipur, strive for the promotion and betterment of Women Engineers and increasing the interest, awareness, and involvement of young females in technical disciplines, especially in the field of Engineering. The mission of WIE MUJ is to facilitate the recruitment and retention of women in technical subjects. The group organizes and hosts diverse activities, including guest speakers, workshops, seminars, technical competitions, and conferences.

  • IEEE MTT-S MUJ

    IEEE Microwave Theory and Techniques Society, Manipal University Jaipur, strives to advance the professional standing of its members and enhance the quality of life for all people through the development and application of microwave technology. As we enter into an exciting future, our mission is to continue to understand and influence microwave technology and provide a forum for all aspiring engineers. Our society offers an excellent opportunity for networking with experienced innovators, experts, and practitioners to gain comprehensive technical knowledge in this domain.

RAJASTHAN SUBSECTION CONGRESS

(RSC)

The RAJASTHAN SUBSECTION CONGRESS 2020 was held on 12th January 2020. The registrations started at 9:30 AM and nearly 150 delegates from nearly 20 colleges including Manipal University Jaipur, Government Engineering College(GEC) Ajmer, Mody University, Chandigarh University, BITS Pilani, IIT Jodhpur, Poornima University, The NorthCap University, Manav Rachna University got themselves registered and got their delegate kits and IDs.


Several Keynote Sessions were conducted by various renowned speakers including Dr. Gulshan Rai who is Chief Cyber Security Officer at PMO , John Boggala who is a product manager at Amazon and south asia coordinator of IEEE R10 young professionals , Shubham Singh the founder of Gramiksha and Dr.Manpreet Kaur.


Refreshments were served throughtout the event and the event ended with some mesmerizing dance and song performances by the students of Manipal University Jaipur.

AI in Space

by Shreyansh Daftry

After having organized its first Webinar, which was highly appreciated, IEEE SB MUJ hosted another impressive webinar - "A Force for Space: Artificial Intelligence" - with a thumping participation of over 600 people.


Technology is bringing us closer and closer to the stars – with Artificial Intelligence leading the way.


Our speaker, Mr. Shreyansh Daftry, is a Research Scientist at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory and is pushing through the boundaries of technology with innovations in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Autonomous Robotics. Mr. Daftry, a master of words, took everyone on a virtual tour to space and decoded the world of artificial intelligence and its importance in space projects. He successfully bridged the gap between space and the modern world.

Open Gates To Success During Lockdown Times

By Manish Tyagi

We organized codeathon named codephrenia in collaboration with codechef. It was organized on codechef itself. The codeathon was an hour and half long and had 4 questions. The winner was awarded with certificate , cash prize of Rs.500 and 250 codechef laddoos.


In the wake of a pandemic that is grappling humanity and is causing widespread concern, fear, and stress due to ever-changing situations, this talk was the need of the hour. Mr. Manish shared his life experiences and enabled us to learn:

  • The art of being successful in these stressful times
  • To use our leadership skills in these testing times
  • To be innovative and productive in lockdown
